# Tip: Mark a Key Project (Favorite)

‍

Too many projects to sift through? **Star** the ones you use often, then filter by "Following" on the home page to find them fast.

## How

- On a home-page project card, click the **star in its top-left corner** to favorite / unfavorite.

![image](https://serve.nutshellai.cn/publish/auto/tutorial/tip-follow.jpg)

## Where to Find Favorites

- Switch the home-page filter to **"Following"** (关注) to see only your favorited projects.
- Stars are saved under your account, so they persist across devices.

> Click the star again to unfavorite.
