# Change a Member's Avatar

‍

The right side of the team panel is a 3D canvas where every member stands on a stage. Use the "**Avatar**" dropdown in the top toolbar to restyle the whole team — it's purely visual and does not affect agent behavior.

## How

- The "**Avatar**" dropdown in the team panel's top-right offers 7 looks: block robot / crystal core / double helix / space droid / jellyfish / mushroom / penguin.
- The adjacent "**Scene**" dropdown switches between 9 backgrounds (city skyline / research lab / deep-space port …).

![image](https://serve.nutshellai.cn/publish/auto/tutorial/tut24-avatar.jpg)

!!! tip
    Avatar and scene are presentation only. The chief stays green, assistants blue, and the selected member is highlighted — handy for clicking the member you're editing directly on the canvas.
